## Full description (recommended)

**Complete secondary maths schemes of work for Key Stage 3, GCSE Foundation and GCSE Higher** — long- and medium-term plans you can use straight away, designed by a Head of Mathematics.

Every topic is sequenced so prior knowledge, key concepts, common misconceptions and success criteria are clear before you teach. Lessons draw on and extend previous learning, so pace and challenge stay consistent across classes and teachers.

### What’s included
- Fully mapped **KS3**, **Foundation GCSE** and **Higher GCSE** pathways
- **Pedagogy notes**, key concepts, common misconceptions and success criteria for each topic
- Links to **ready-to-teach lessons** and differentiated worksheets on Mr Mathematics
- Suitable for **Edexcel, AQA, OCR and WJEC** (and parallel UK/international GCSE-style courses)

### Who it’s for
- Heads of Maths who need a coherent department plan
- Classroom teachers who want next week planned without rebuilding schemes from scratch
- Tutors and international schools following a UK-style curriculum

### How to use it
Download the schemes, follow the sequence for your year group or tier, and open the linked lessons when you teach each topic. The schemes themselves are free to use for planning; full interactive lessons, PowerPoints and worksheets are available through [Mr Mathematics membership](https://mr-mathematics.com/mr-mathematics-membership/).
