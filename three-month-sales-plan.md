# Mr Mathematics — 3-month full-time sales plan

**Window:** mid-July 2026 → mid-October 2026  
**Context:** UK summer holidays now; teachers/schools rarely buy until **mid/late August**. September historically carries the year (~£2.1–£2.8k in one month).  
**Baseline:** ~£9.3k TTM; Teacher £49.95; School was £119.50, now listed **£395**.  
**SEO state:** Recovering from March crawl blackout (~⅓ of Feb impressions); sitewide CTR **0.8%**; Cloudflare now allows Google.

---

## Direct answer: pause Resource of the Day?

**Yes — pause weekly ROTD updates for ~3 weeks (roughly now → ~6–8 August).** That is the right call.

| Why pause | Why not longer |
|-----------|----------------|
| UK purchase intent is near-zero in late July | You already have a strong “Getting September Ready” theme live — leave it up |
| ROTD is a weekly content treadmill with weak mid-holiday conversion | Resume **week of 11 Aug** (or 18 Aug at latest) with a September-planning theme |
| Full-time hours are better spent on index recovery, CTR, and blog refurbs that compound into September | Don’t kill the URL — keep the page published; just stop new weekly cycles |

**Do not** spend July only writing long blogs while ignoring reindex + title/meta. Your refurbished [FDP guide](https://mr-mathematics.com/fractions-decimals-and-percentages/) is the right *format*, but GSC still shows **~0.12% CTR** at position ~2.7 — content quality alone has not unlocked clicks. Every refurb must include **SERP title/meta + membership CTA**, not just a longer article.

---

## 3-month outcome targets (realistic stretch)

Not “hit £55k in 90 days” — use the quarter to reverse the decline and set up a strong autumn.

| Metric | Today (approx.) | End of Oct target |
|--------|----------------:|------------------:|
| Google impressions / month | ~0.45–0.5M (Jun) | **≥0.9M** (toward Jan–Feb band) |
| Average CTR (Search) | 0.8% | **≥1.2%** on refurbished + title-fixed set |
| Teacher memberships (Aug–Oct) | historically soft in Aug, strong Sep | **≥90 units** in the quarter (~£4.5k) |
| School @ £395 | ~0 visible in old £119 reports | **≥8 closed** (~£3.2k) + pipeline of 20 |
| Quarter membership revenue | — | **£8–12k** (beat prior Aug–Oct run-rate) |

If Sep alone beats **£3k**, you are ahead of history.

## Month 1 — 16 Jul → 15 Aug: “Recover & rebuild (low sales expected)”

### Theme
Win back Google visibility and prepare assets for September. **Do not judge this month on revenue.**

### Pause
- Weekly **Resource of the Day** new themes (leave current September-ready week live).
- New freebie volume that isn’t tied to a refurb or membership path.

### Must-do (non-negotiable)

1. **Finish crawl recovery**
   - Validate fix on GSC 403 issue  
   - Work through `reindex-priority-50` (~8 URLs/day until done, then next 50 from 403 export by clicks)  
   - Weekly check: crawl stats + impressions trend  

2. **CTR harvest before / alongside refurbs**
   - Each week: fix titles + meta on top high-impression / low-CTR URLs (content *and* `/product/` where thin titles hurt)  
   - Pattern: `[Topic] — KS3/GCSE Lesson, Worksheet & Teacher Guide | Mr Mathematics`  
   - Your FDP page and IGCSE FDP hub (~24k / ~82k impr, &lt;0.15% CTR) need title tests immediately  

3. **Blog refurb pipeline (your plan — approved with targeting rules)**  
   Format = [FDP example](https://mr-mathematics.com/fractions-decimals-and-percentages/): teacher’s guide, step-by-step, free worksheet, exam Qs, FAQ, **clear membership / lesson-pack CTA**.  

   **Only refurb pages that already rank** (impressions), especially low CTR. Priority queue from GSC:

   | Priority | URL (examples) | Why |
   |----------|----------------|-----|
   | 1 | `/area-of-compound-shapes/` | 175k impr, 0.83% CTR |
   | 2 | `/interpreting-cumulative-frequency-graphs/` | 116k impr, 0.28% CTR |
   | 3 | `/plan-and-elevation-drawings-of-3d-shapes/` | 111k impr, 0.42% CTR |
   | 4 | `/calculating-the-volume-of-a-pyramid/` | 129k impr, 0.03% CTR |
   | 5 | `/igcse-mathematics-foundation-fractions-decimals-and-percentages/` | 82k impr, 0.08% CTR — align with your new FDP guide |
   | 6 | `/vector-equation-of-a-plane/` | 108k impr, in 403 list |
   | 7 | `/sine-rule-in-gcse-mathematics/` | 90k impr, tiny CTR |
   | 8 | Other IGCSE foundation hubs with pos ~1–3 and CTR &lt;0.3% | Easy wins |

   Cadence: **2 full refurbs per week** (not 5 shallow ones). Each refurb ships with: new title/meta, internal links to schemes + membership, worksheet PDF, teacher guide section.

4. **Conversion prep (quiet month — build the machine)**
   - Membership page: Teacher vs School (£395) crystal clear; explain what changed from £119  
   - School one-pager + PO form path ready  
   - Zoho segments: active / lapsed / free / multi-teacher domains  
   - Draft September email sequence (4 emails) now, send from mid-Aug  

5. **School price story**
   - Document publicly: School **£395** = whole-department access; Teacher **£49.95** = individual  
   - Decide: any courtesy path for schools that paid £119 in the last 12 months? (e.g. renew once at £199 or credit) — reduces backlash, optional  
   - Do **not** quietly leave £119.50 purchasable if £395 is the real product  

### Month 1 success = 
Impressions rising week-on-week; 8 refurbished URLs live; 403 Validate Fix in progress; September email + school pack ready. Sales can stay near-zero.

## Month 2 — 16 Aug → 15 Sep: “Sell September”

### Theme
Teachers come back to planning. This is where full-time work must turn into **basket adds**.

### Resume ROTD
- Restart **week of 11 or 18 August** with themes: *Week 1 lessons*, *Settling Year 7*, *GCSE retrieval*, *Department consistency*.  
- Every ROTD week ends in membership + schemes CTA (you already do this — keep it sharp).

### Must-do

1. **Launch campaign (live by 20 Aug)**
   - Homepage + schemes hub + membership: “Plan September in a day”  
   - Offer: Teacher £49.95 instant access; School £395 PO  
   - Optional: early-bird School bonus (e.g. 30-min HoD onboarding call) — not a discount war  

2. **Email (Zoho) — weekly, non-negotiable**
   - 20 Aug: September-ready unit (premium vs free path)  
   - 27 Aug: Schemes of work + first fortnight  
   - 3 Sep: Lapsed win-back  
   - 10 Sep: School / department upgrade for multi-seat schools  

3. **School outreach (4–5 hrs/week)**
   - 10 personal HoD emails/week  
   - Target: schools already using free schemes; domains with 2+ teacher accounts  
   - Goal: **8 × £395** closed by mid-Sep or firm POs  

4. **SEO continues, but capped**
   - 1 refurb/week + ongoing title fixes (don’t let content eat sales time)  
   - Reindex any money URL that still shows old March crawl date  

5. **Watch daily in first two weeks of September**
   - Add-to-cart, Teacher checkouts, School enquiries  
   - If carts high / checkouts low → fix checkout friction same day  

### Month 2 success =
September revenue **≥ £2,800** (beat 2024 peak) or clear path via open School POs; Teacher units clearly above Aug 2025 run-rate.

## Month 3 — 16 Sep → 15 Oct: “Convert the spike + lock retention”

### Theme
September buyers need onboarding so they renew; November is the second peak — prepare it.

### Must-do

1. **Onboard September cohort**
   - Email: “Your first three lessons this week” using members’ schemes  
   - Cut support friction (login, downloads)  

2. **School expansion**
   - Ask every new Teacher in a shared school domain about department upgrade  
   - Case study: one HoD quote for the membership page  

3. **Win-back + renewals**
   - Anyone who bought Aug 2025–Oct 2025 → renewal reminder track  
   - Lapsed sequence again before half-term  

4. **SEO**
   - Refurb the next 4–6 high-impression pages  
   - Publish one “what’s new for autumn” hub linking refurbished lessons (internal-link recovery)  

5. **November prep**
   - Exam / mock / data-driven teaching theme ready by mid-Oct  

### Month 3 success =
Quarter revenue on track for **£8–12k**; churn not spiking; impressions ≥2× June; School pipeline not empty after September rush.
