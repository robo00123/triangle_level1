New Free Blogs – Hypothesis Testing

Rather than walking through rote procedures, each blog focuses on understanding the underlying process — why we do what we do at every step. They cover:

  • Key concepts and the logic behind hypothesis testing
  • Correct notation and how to set out hypotheses properly
  • Common mistakes students make (and how to avoid them)
  • How to write a valid, meaningful conclusion in context

Binomial Hypothesis Testing in A-Level Maths

A clear, conceptual walk-through of one- and two-tailed binomial tests, including how to interpret critical regions and write conclusions that genuinely answer the question.

PMCC Hypothesis Testing in A-Level Maths

Normal Distribution Hypothesis Testing in A-Level Maths

Understanding tests on the population mean using the Normal distribution — covering notation, the role of the Central Limit Theorem, and writing precise conclusions.
